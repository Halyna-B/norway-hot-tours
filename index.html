Hot Tours to Norway

Nature Hike in Lofoten

Make the most of your exploration of Austvågøya Island, one of the Lofoten Archipelago's main isles, during this 3-days guided walking tour. Learn about the landscape, flora, and fauna from your guide as you walk.

Bergen: Yacht Cruise

Make the most of your time in port on this shore excursion in Bergen, timed to your yacht. Departing from the center of Bergen, head along the narrow Mostraumen fjord to see some of western Norway's most spectacular scenery.

Mountain Trip - Moskenes municipality

We will visit the famous and breathtaking mountain top, called Ryten in Moskenes municipality in Lofoten.
